# Research Protocol

Every strategy starts as a hypothesis, not a trade.

## Strategy Template

- Hypothesis.
- Economic intuition.
- Asset universe.
- Required data.
- Signal definition.
- Holding period.
- Portfolio construction.
- Risk controls.
- Benchmark.
- Cost and slippage assumptions.
- Validation plan.
- Failure modes.
- Final classification.

## Validation Rules

- Train on past data and test on future data.
- Avoid random K-fold cross validation for time series.
- Track failed experiments.
- Compare to passive baselines.
- Check robustness across regimes.
- Treat LLM-generated ideas as untrusted hypotheses.

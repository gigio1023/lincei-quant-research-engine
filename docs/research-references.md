## How To Use References

Use references to design experiments and contracts, not to copy blindly.

For each adopted idea, record:

- source;
- what was adopted;
- what was changed for this project;
- validation plan;
- failure modes.

## Practical Conclusions

- LEAN gives the executable trading language and backtest/runtime semantics.
- Numeric alpha remains essential for repeatable price-volume and portfolio signals.
- LLM alpha is now strong enough to be a first-class judgment source for text-heavy events.
- LLM decisions need typed schemas, evidence refs, counter-thesis, and latency records.
- Portfolio sizing should remain deterministic or trained/calibrated, not free-form LLM output.
- Backtests must store failed trials and guard against lookahead and selection bias.
